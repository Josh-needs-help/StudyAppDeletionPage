Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your AceIt account and associated data, please follow the instructions below:

How to Request Deletion

Email us at aceitapphelp@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Be sure to include the email address or username associated with your account.

What Happens Next

  • Your account and personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.
  • Certain data (such as financial or legal records) may be retained for compliance purposes.
  • After deletion, your account cannot be recovered.

If you have questions, please contact us at aceitapphelp@gmail.com.
